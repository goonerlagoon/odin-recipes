Corned Beef & Bread

The real National Dish of Jamaica

Ingredients

  • 1 can of corned beef
  • X slices of bread. (where X is even and generally varies between 2 and 10 but dont let this recipe hold you back buddy)
  • Some onion
  • A likkle mayo

Steps

  1. Untie your bread bag, posthaste.
  2. Retrieve X/2 slices and lay them bastards in a line on your chopping board.
  3. Mayo them. Graciously.
  4. Plop a healthy amount (read: 2 tbsps) of corned beef on the mayod slices and spread with earnest.
  5. This step is optional but highly recommended for an unforgettable experience: take the remaining X/2 slices, AND MAYO THEM SUCKERS TOO
  6. When done, take the slices and slap them on top of your ALREADY delightful but imcomplete concoction.
  7. Feast.
